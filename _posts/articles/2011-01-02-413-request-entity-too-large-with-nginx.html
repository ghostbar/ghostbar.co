After a little bit of googling I found out what was happening: What needs to be done is on /etc/nginx/nginx.conf write: 
http {
    client_max_body_size 20M;
    include       /etc/nginx/mime.types;
    A LOT MORE SETTINGS...
Yes, just add client_max_body_size and restart your webserver. This by default is 1M, so anything beyond this will be refused.
